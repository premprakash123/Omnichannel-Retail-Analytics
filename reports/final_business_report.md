# 📊 Omnichannel Retail Analytics – Final Business Report

## Executive Summary
This project analyzes omnichannel retail sales performance across multiple Indian cities using Python, SQL, and Power BI. The objective was to identify revenue trends, product performance, customer purchase behavior, and city-wise opportunities. The dashboard reveals strong growth momentum, high dependence on Electronics, and clear opportunities for improving Average Order Value (AOV) through targeted city strategies.

---

## Methodology
The project followed a three-stage analytics pipeline:

1. **Python (Data Cleaning & Preparation)**  
   - Cleaned raw sales data  
   - Handled missing values  
   - Standardized formats  

2. **SQL (Business Analysis)**  
   - Revenue aggregation  
   - Product/category analysis  
   - Order trends  
   - City-wise KPIs  

3. **Power BI (Visualization Dashboard)**  
   - KPI Cards  
   - Revenue by Product  
   - City Analysis  
   - Seasonality Trends  
   - Units Sold Dashboard  

---

## Key Findings

### 💰 Overall Performance
- Total Revenue reached **₹8.51M**
- Total Orders recorded: **19K**
- Overall business growth: **+6% MoM**
- Average Order Value (AOV): **₹448.98**

### 🛍️ Product Performance
- Electronics contributes **68%–70%** of total revenue
- Home Decor is the second strongest category
- Beauty contributes around **8%**
- Grocery and Clothing remain smaller but stable categories

### 🌆 City Insights
- **Hyderabad** has highest revenue: **₹1.30M**
- **Delhi** strong performer: **₹1.26M**
- **Kolkata** stable revenue: **₹1.21M**
- **Bengaluru** revenue: **₹1.19M**
- **Chennai** lowest revenue: **₹1.13M**

### 🧾 AOV Analysis
- Hyderabad highest AOV: **₹475**
- Delhi AOV: **₹463**
- Kolkata AOV: **₹448**
- Bengaluru AOV: **₹437**
- Chennai lowest AOV: **₹431**

### 📦 Units Sold Trends
- Demand is balanced across cities
- Electronics leads units sold in most cities
- Some cities show stronger Home Decor / Beauty performance

### 📅 Seasonality
- Q4 contributes approximately **32%–35%** of yearly revenue
- Strong spikes during festive months
- Opportunity for pre-season campaigns

---

## Actionable Recommendations

### 🚀 Revenue Growth
- Expand Electronics inventory and premium variants
- Launch accessory bundles with electronics

### 🎯 City Strategy
- Focus aggressive growth campaigns in Bengaluru
- Use retention offers in Delhi and Hyderabad
- Improve Chennai basket size using combo offers

### 🛒 AOV Improvement
- Introduce minimum-spend free shipping
- Add upsell suggestions at checkout
- Create curated product bundles

### 🎉 Seasonal Planning
- Start festive campaigns in October
- Increase inventory before Q4 demand surge
- Offer early-bird discounts

### 🔁 Customer Retention
- Loyalty points program
- Repeat purchase coupons for Beauty/Grocery
- Personalized offers based on city behavior

---

## Conclusion
The business is growing steadily with strong category leadership from Electronics and high-value opportunities in premium markets like Hyderabad. By applying city-specific pricing, improving low-AOV markets, and preparing early for festive demand, the company can significantly increase profitability and customer lifetime value.
