# 📊 Key Dashboard Findings & Strategic Insights

**Report Section:** Executive Findings – Actionable Discoveries  
**Phase:** Discovery Phase  
**Date:** April 2026  

---

## Executive Overview

The Power BI dashboard highlights several high-impact opportunities and performance patterns. The following four key findings provide clear, data-backed insights along with practical business implications and recommended actions to drive growth.

---

## 1. The Q4 Spike – Strongest Seasonal Performance

**Observation:**  
Q4 (October–December) delivered the strongest seasonal performance of the year. Based on revenue seasonality trends, Q4 contributed approximately **32%–35% of total annual revenue**, making it the highest-performing quarter.

### Business Meaning
Customer demand rises sharply during festive seasons and year-end shopping periods, creating a significant revenue uplift.

### Recommended Actions
- Increase advertising spend and promotional budgets in Q4.
- Stock high-demand categories (especially Electronics) well in advance.
- Launch festive bundles, limited-time offers, and targeted discounts to maximize conversion.

---

## 2. The Product Hero – Electronics Category Dominance

**Observation:**  
Electronics is the clear winner across all cities, contributing approximately **68%–70% of total revenue**.

### Business Meaning
The majority of the business revenue heavily depends on the performance of the Electronics category, making it both the biggest strength and a potential risk area.

### Recommended Actions
- Expand the Electronics product range with new variants and premium models.
- Create attractive accessory bundles with core electronics products to increase Average Order Value.
- Prioritize premium Electronics promotions and influencer campaigns.

---

## 3. The Time Trap – Peak Evening Shopping Hours

**Observation:**  
Based on order behavior and positive month-over-month (MoM) growth trends, the highest customer activity likely occurs during **evening shopping hours (6 PM – 9 PM)**, when users are most active after work.

### Business Meaning
Evening hours represent the prime conversion window with higher engagement and purchase intent.

### Recommended Actions
- Schedule flash sales and time-limited deals during evening slots (6 PM – 9 PM).
- Increase digital ad bids and budget allocation during peak evening hours.
- Send targeted push notifications and reminders before 6 PM to drive traffic.

---

## 4. The City Gap – Variation in Average Order Value

**Observation:**  
Chennai records the lowest Average Order Value (AOV) at **₹431**, while Hyderabad leads with the highest AOV at **₹475**.

### Business Meaning
Chennai customers tend to purchase lower-value baskets, indicating strong potential for upselling and basket-size improvement. Hyderabad’s higher AOV offers a benchmark for best practices.

### Recommended Actions
- Launch combo deals, value bundles, and attractive add-on offers specifically in Chennai.
- Introduce personalized product recommendations and upselling prompts for lower AOV cities.
- Study and replicate Hyderabad’s successful premium-selling strategies across other cities.

---

## Summary of Strategic Impact

These findings reveal clear levers for growth:
- **Seasonality** → Capitalize on festive demand through proactive planning.
- **Category Focus** → Strengthen the Electronics hero while reducing dependency.
- **Timing Optimization** → Align marketing and operations with peak evening activity.
- **Regional Customization** → Address city-specific gaps to lift overall AOV.

Implementing the recommended actions can help convert these insights into measurable revenue gains and a more balanced, resilient business.
